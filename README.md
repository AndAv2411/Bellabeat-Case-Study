## ðŸ“Š Key Findings & Analysis
After processing the FitBit Fitness Tracker Data (Public Domain), the following trends were identified:

### 1. Sedentary Lifestyle vs. Sleep Quality
There is a clear **negative correlation** between sedentary minutes and sleep duration. Users who spend more time inactive during the day tend to sleep less at night.
* *Average Sedentary Time:* 712 minutes (~11.8 hours/day).
* *Average Sleep:* 419 minutes (~7 hours).

### 2. Device Usage Consistency
* **30% of users** (9 out of 33) stop using the tracking device at night. This presents a massive opportunity to market a more comfortable device for sleep tracking.

### 3. Activity Levels
* Users average **8,515 steps**, falling short of the standard 10,000-step health recommendation.
